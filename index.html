ABOUT

Khalia is a soulful music artist of Hmong ethnicity whose sound blends heartfelt melodies with raw, resonant storytelling. Rooted in her heritage and shaped by her journey through an array of genres such as pop, R&B, country, and traditional Hmong music, her artistry explores themes of identity, resilience, and self-discovery, bridging past and present in a style that is both timeless and innovative.


From underground performances and competitions to hosting her first concert in 2024, Khalia has consistently captivated audiences with her powerful voice and authentic presence. Whether on stage or in the studio, she creates music that connects deeply, celebrating personal truth while resonating with universal emotions.
