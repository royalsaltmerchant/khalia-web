ABOUT

Khalia is a Bay Area artist with a voice that stops you mid-conversation. Drawing from soul, pop, and the kind of storytelling that feels personal even when it isn't yours, she creates music rooted in honesty and built to last.


Of Hmong heritage, Khalia brings a perspective that is rarely heard in mainstream music, one shaped by identity, resilience, and the tension between where you come from and who you're becoming.


She has headlined her own concerts and performed at festivals and events across the Bay Area, consistently delivering live shows backed by a full band. Her performances are known for their emotional depth and the kind of presence that stays with you long after the last note.
